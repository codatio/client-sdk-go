For example: <br/><br/>- When a journal entry is recording a receipt of cash, the credit to accounts receivable and the debit to cash are separate line items. <br/>- When a company needs to recognise revenue from an annual contract on a monthly basis, on receipt of cash for month one, they make a debit to deferred income and a credit to revenue.<br/><br/>In Codat a journal entry contains details of:<br/><br/>- The date on which the entry was created and posted.<br/>- Itemised lines, including amounts and currency.<br/>- A reference to the associated accounts.<br/>- A reference to the underlying record.
